About Yvonne

Hi, I'm Yvonne Padilla — the person behind Digital Navigation Solutions.

I spent 27 years in education, 17 of them as a school principal. That job taught me a lot of things, but the part I loved most — and the part I was best at — was building relationships. Helping a family navigate a hard decision. Figuring out what a student actually needed and finding a way to get it for them. Leading a team of people toward something better than where we started.

Digital Navigation Solutions is me doing the same thing, just in a different room.

I started DNS because I kept meeting smart, capable small business owners who were paying too much for a website that didn't work for them — or paying a monthly fee for a site they couldn't even update themselves. That's not how this should work. I build simple, beautifully designed websites on modern technology, and I don't charge you a dime in hosting fees after launch. You own your site. You control your content. I'm just the person who makes sure it all runs beautifully in the background.

If you need a full website, I can build you one. If you just need help connecting a domain, or escaping a platform you've outgrown, I can do that too. And if you're a small business owner who keeps hearing about AI but has no idea where to start — let's talk. I promise I'll make it make sense.

Based in Colorado Springs. Working with businesses of every size — from solopreneurs to established teams — if you want to work with one person who knows your name, we'll get along.
